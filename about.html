About Us

Our Story

Passion rooted in
every petal

Helen Florist has been Sterling Heights' trusted floral studio, dedicated to transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through the timeless art of flowers.

Our florists hand-select the freshest blooms — blending classical elegance with contemporary flair to create arrangements as unique as the people who receive them. We believe beautiful flowers should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer stunning quality at prices that never compromise your budget.

From the moment you walk through our doors to the moment your arrangement is delivered, we prioritize your vision, your joy, and your satisfaction above all else.

Quality First

Only the freshest, most vibrant blooms make it into our studio — hand-selected with care every single day.

Affordable Luxury

Stunning florals that respect your budget — because everyone deserves beautiful flowers without compromise.

Personal Touch

Every arrangement is a collaboration — our expert florists work closely with you to bring your vision to life.
